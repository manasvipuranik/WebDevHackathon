Where Street Food Dreams Come Alive

Connecting local street food vendors with suppliers, and opportunities. From cart to community – we're building India's largest street food ecosystem.

Empowering vendors with reliable sourcing, visibility, and community support to thrive in the food streets of Nagpur.
